# Welcome

Welcome to **HyCloud Hosting**!

We're excited to have you here.

Whether you're hosting a Minecraft server, a Discord bot, or any future service, this documentation is designed to help you get started quickly and make the most of your hosting experience.

Our guides are written for both beginners and experienced users, providing step-by-step instructions, best practices, and troubleshooting tips along the way.

---

## Before You Begin

Before continuing, make sure you have:

- A registered HyCloud Hosting account.
- Access to the Client Area.
- An active hosting service (or be ready to order one).
- Access to the email address associated with your account.

These requirements ensure you can follow every guide in this documentation without interruptions.
