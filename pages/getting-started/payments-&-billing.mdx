# Payments & Billing

HyCloud Hosting uses a secure billing platform that allows you to manage your services, invoices, and payments from a single place.

All billing operations are handled through the Client Area.

---

## Accepted Payment Methods

Currently, we accept payments through:

- PayPal
- Credit and Debit Cards (processed securely through PayPal)

Additional payment methods may be introduced in the future.

---

## Paying an Invoice

When you place an order, an invoice will be generated automatically.

To pay an invoice:

1. Log in to the Client Area.
2. Open **Invoices**.
3. Select the unpaid invoice.
4. Click **Pay Now**.
5. Complete the payment through PayPal.

Once the payment has been confirmed, your service will begin provisioning automatically.

---

## Invoice Status

Invoices can have different statuses:

| Status | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Paid | The invoice has been successfully paid. |
| Unpaid | Payment is still required. |
| Cancelled | The invoice has been cancelled and is no longer payable. |
| Refunded | The payment has been refunded according to our Refund Policy. |

---

## Automatic Service Suspension

Services that remain unpaid after their due date may be suspended automatically.

To restore a suspended service, simply pay the outstanding invoice. Once the payment is confirmed, the service will be reactivated automatically whenever possible.

---

## Viewing Billing History

The Client Area allows you to review your billing history at any time.

You can view:

- Paid invoices
- Pending invoices
- Invoice dates
- Payment history
- Downloadable PDF invoices (when available)
