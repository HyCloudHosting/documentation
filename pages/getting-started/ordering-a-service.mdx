# Ordering a Service

Ordering a service with HyCloud Hosting is quick and straightforward. You can purchase any available service from our website or directly from the Client Area.

## Option 1 — From our Website (Recommended)

The easiest way to order a service is through our website.

1. Visit the Services section:

   https://hycloudhosting.com/#services

2. Choose the hosting service that best fits your needs.

Available services include:

- Minecraft Hosting
- Hytale Hosting
- Discord Bot Hosting

3. Click **View Plans**.

4. Select the plan you want.

5. You will be redirected to the Client Area to complete your order.

---

## Option 2 — From the Client Area

If you already have an account, you can order a new service directly from the Billing Area.

1. Log in to the Client Area.

2. Click **Shop**.

3. Choose the category and product you wish to purchase.

4. Configure your service according to your needs.

5. Continue to checkout and complete the payment.

The Billing Area is available at:

https://billing.hycloudhosting.com

---

## Configuring Your Service

Before completing your order, you may be asked to configure several options depending on the selected service.

Examples include:

- Server location
- Server Name
- Additional services or upgrades

The available options vary depending on the selected product.

---

## Payment

Once your configuration is complete, you will be taken to the checkout page.

Review your order carefully before confirming the payment.

After the payment has been successfully processed, your service will begin provisioning automatically.

Provisioning times vary depending on the selected product.
