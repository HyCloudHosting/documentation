# Opening a Support Ticket

If you need assistance with one of your services, the fastest and most effective way to contact our team is by opening a support ticket.

Support tickets allow us to track your request, keep all communication organized, and provide the best possible assistance.

---

## Before Opening a Ticket

Before contacting support, we recommend checking the documentation to see if your question has already been answered.

You may find a solution faster without waiting for a response.

---

## Creating a Support Ticket in the Client Area

To create a new support ticket:

1. Log in to the [Client Area](https://billing.hycloudhosting.com/tickets).
2. Navigate to **Tickets**.
3. Click **Open Ticket**.
4. Select the department that best matches your request.
5. Enter a descriptive subject.
6. Explain your issue with as much detail as possible.
7. Attach screenshots or files if they help explain the problem.
8. Submit the ticket.

Once submitted, our team will review your request as soon as possible.

---

## Creating a Support Ticket in Discord

Discord is the fastest and recommended way to receive support.

To open a support ticket through Discord:

1. Join the official HyCloud Hosting [Discord](https://discord.hycloudhosting.com) server.
2. Navigate to the **Tickets** channel.
3. Select the category that best matches your request.
4. A private support ticket will be created automatically.
5. Describe your issue with as much detail as possible.
6. Attach screenshots or files if necessary.

Our support team actively monitors Discord tickets, making this the quickest way to receive assistance.

---

## Writing a Good Support Ticket

Providing detailed information helps us resolve your issue faster.

A good support ticket should include:

- A clear description of the problem.
- What you were trying to do.
- Any error messages you received.
- Screenshots (if applicable).
- The affected service.
- The approximate time the issue occurred.

The more information you provide, the easier it will be for our team to investigate.

---

## Response Times

Support requests are handled in the order they are received, although urgent service interruptions may receive higher priority.

Response times may vary depending on:

- Ticket priority
- Complexity of the issue
- Current support workload

---

## Updating Your Ticket

After submitting a ticket, you can:

- Reply with additional information.
- Upload new attachments.
- Review previous responses.
- Track the status of your request.

You will receive email notifications whenever our team replies to your ticket.

---

## Ticket Statuses

Your ticket may display one of the following statuses:

| Status | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Open | Your ticket has been received and is awaiting review. |
| Answered | Our team has responded and is waiting for your reply. |
| Customer Reply | You have replied and the ticket is waiting for our team. |
| Closed | The issue has been resolved and the ticket has been closed. |

---

## Emergency Situations

If you believe your service is experiencing a critical outage, please clearly describe the impact in your support ticket.

Our team will investigate the issue as quickly as possible.

---

## Need More Help?

If you are unable to access the Client Area, you can also reach us through our official Discord community.

Please note that account-specific requests may still require verification through the Client Area for security reasons.
