# Client Area

The Client Area is the central place where you can manage all your HyCloud Hosting services.

Using the Client Area, you can:

- Order new services
- View active services
- Manage invoices
- Open support tickets
- Access your game servers
- Update your personal information
- Download invoices
- Track your order history

## Accessing the Client Area

You can access the Client Area at:

https://billing.hycloudhosting.com

Once you have logged in, you will be able to manage all your services from a single dashboard.

## Dashboard

The dashboard provides a quick overview of:

- Active Services
- Pending Invoices
- Recent Orders
- Support Tickets
- Account Balance (if applicable)

## Security

For your security, always:

- Use a strong password.
- Never share your account credentials.
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication when available.

## Need help?

If you experience any issues accessing your account, contact our Support Team through the Client Area or join our Discord server.
