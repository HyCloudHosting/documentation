# Ordering a Hytale Server

Ordering a Hytale server through HyCloud Hosting is quick and straightforward.

Once your order has been completed and payment has been confirmed, your server will be automatically deployed and ready to manage from the control panel.

---

## Choosing a Plan

Begin by selecting the Hytale hosting plan that best fits your needs.

When comparing plans, consider:

- Available RAM
- CPU allocation
- NVMe SSD storage
- Expected number of players
- Future expansion

If you're unsure which plan is right for your community, our support team can help you choose the most suitable option.

---

## Placing Your Order

To order a Hytale server:

1. Visit the HyCloud Hosting website.
2. Navigate to **Game Servers**.
3. Select **Hytale**.
4. Choose the hosting plan that best fits your requirements.
5. Configure your order if additional options are available.
6. Complete the checkout process.

Once payment has been confirmed, your deployment will begin automatically.

---

## Server Deployment

After your payment has been processed:

- Your server is automatically created.
- Required files are installed.
- The control panel account is linked to your service.
- The server becomes available in your Client Area.

Deployment is usually completed within a few minutes.

---

## Accessing Your Server

Once deployment is complete, you can access your server through the Pterodactyl Control Panel.

From there you can:

- Start and stop the server.
- Access the live console.
- Manage server files.
- Monitor resource usage.
- Create backups.

Detailed instructions are available in the following guides.

---

## Before Inviting Players

Before sharing your server address, we recommend:

- Starting the server at least once.
- Checking the console for any errors.
- Verifying that the server is running correctly.
- Testing the connection yourself.

This helps ensure your server is ready for players.

---

## Need Help?

If you experience any issues while ordering or deploying your Hytale server, our support team is available through Discord and the Client Area.
