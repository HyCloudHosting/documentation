# Subusers

Subusers allow you to give other people access to your Minecraft server without sharing your own HyCloud Hosting or Pterodactyl account.

Instead of giving someone your login credentials, you can invite them as a subuser and choose exactly what they are allowed to do.

This is the recommended way to share server management with friends, moderators, developers, or administrators.

---

## Why Use Subusers?

Using subusers is much safer than sharing your account password.

Benefits include:

- Keep your account secure.
- Control what each user can access.
- Remove access at any time.
- Give different permissions to different people.
- Track who has access to your server.

---

## Creating a Subuser

To invite someone:

1. Open your Minecraft server.
2. Navigate to **Subusers**.
3. Click **Create New**.
4. Enter the user's email address.
5. Select the permissions you want to grant.
6. Click **Invite User**.

The invited user will receive an invitation to access the server.

---

## Permission Management

When creating a subuser, you can choose which sections of the panel they can access.

Examples include:

- Console
- File Manager
- Backups
- Databases
- Schedules
- Activity

Grant only the permissions necessary for each user.

---

## Editing Permissions

Permissions can be modified at any time.

If a user's responsibilities change, simply edit their assigned permissions without creating a new invitation.

---

## Removing a Subuser

If someone no longer needs access:

1. Open **Subusers**.
2. Select the user.
3. Click **Delete**.
4. Confirm the removal.

Their access to your server will be revoked immediately.

---

## Best Practices

To keep your server secure, we recommend:

- Only invite people you trust.
- Grant the minimum permissions required.
- Remove inactive subusers.
- Review permissions periodically.
- Never share your own account credentials.

---

## Common Use Cases

Subusers are commonly used for:

- Server administrators
- Moderators
- Plugin developers
- Builders
- Friends helping manage a community

Each person can receive only the permissions they actually need.

---

## Important Notes

Subusers only have access to the servers you explicitly share with them.

They cannot access:

- Your Client Area.
- Billing information.
- Other servers in your account.
- Your HyCloud Hosting account settings.

Their access is limited to the permissions you assign.
