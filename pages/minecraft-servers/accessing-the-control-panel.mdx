# Accessing the Control Panel

Every Minecraft server includes access to the Pterodactyl Control Panel, where you can manage every aspect of your server.

From the control panel, you can:

- Start, stop and restart your server.
- Access the live console.
- Manage files.
- Create backups.
- Configure startup variables.
- Monitor resource usage.
- Schedule automated tasks.
- And much more.

---

## First Login

For security reasons, you should create your own panel password before accessing the control panel for the first time.

To do this:

1. Log in to the Client Area.
2. Open your Minecraft service.
3. Click **Login to Panel**.
4. Select **Forgot Password**.
5. Enter the email address associated with your HyCloud Hosting account.
6. Follow the instructions sent to your email.
7. Create your new password.

Once completed, you can sign in to the control panel using:

- Your email address
- Your newly created password

---

## Connected Accounts

After accessing your panel, you can connect third-party accounts to make future logins easier.

Supported providers include:

- Google
- Discord
- GitHub

To manage your connected accounts:

1. Log in to the Pterodactyl Control Panel.
2. Open **Account Settings**.
3. Navigate to **Connected Accounts**.
4. Link the providers you wish to use.

You can access the Connected Accounts page directly at:

https://panel.hycloudhosting.com/account

Once linked, you can sign in using any connected provider without entering your panel password.

> **Note**
>
> Connecting external accounts is optional. You can continue using your email address and password if you prefer.

---

## Accessing the Panel

After setting your password:

1. Log in to the Client Area.
2. Open your Minecraft server.
3. Click **Login to Panel**.

If you are already signed in, you will be automatically redirected to your server.

If your session has expired, simply log in again using your panel credentials.

---

## Multiple Servers

If your account contains multiple Minecraft servers, all of them will appear inside the same control panel account.

You can easily switch between servers using the server selector available in the panel.

---

## Keeping Your Account Secure

To help protect your account, we recommend:

- Using a strong and unique password.
- Never sharing your login credentials.
- Logging out when using a shared computer.
- Keeping your email account secure, since password resets are sent there.

---

## Having Trouble Accessing the Panel?

If you cannot access your control panel after resetting your password, please open a support ticket through our Discord server.

Our team will help you regain access as quickly as possible.
