# Creating Your Server

Creating a Minecraft server with HyCloud Hosting only takes a few minutes.

During the ordering process, you will be able to customize your server before it is automatically deployed to your account.

---

## Step 1 — Choose a Plan

Visit our website and navigate to the Minecraft hosting plans.

Select the plan that best fits your project based on the amount of RAM and resources you need.

If you are unsure which plan to choose, you can always upgrade later.

---

## Step 2 — Configure Your Server

After selecting a plan, you'll be asked to configure your server.

Depending on the available options, you may choose:

- Server name
- Minecraft Edition (Java or Bedrock)
- Server software
- Server location (if multiple locations are available)
- Billing cycle

Review your configuration before continuing.

---

## Step 3 — Complete Your Order

After configuring your server:

1. Review your order.
2. Complete the payment.
3. Wait for the payment to be confirmed.

Once your payment has been processed, your server will automatically begin provisioning.

---

## Step 4 — Automatic Deployment

HyCloud Hosting automatically creates your server after your order is completed.

This process includes:

- Creating your server
- Installing the selected software
- Configuring the server environment
- Creating your Pterodactyl account (if necessary)
- Linking the server to your Client Area

No manual setup is required.

---

## Step 5 — Access Your Server

When deployment is complete, you can access your server from the Client Area.

Simply:

1. Log in to your account.
2. Open **Services**.
3. Select your Minecraft server.
4. Click **Login to Panel**.

You will be redirected to the Pterodactyl Control Panel where you can fully manage your server.

---

## First Startup

The first startup may take slightly longer than usual while Minecraft generates the server files.

This is completely normal.

Once the server finishes starting, you can connect using the server IP displayed in the control panel.

---

## Need Help?

If your server is taking longer than expected to deploy or you experience any issues during the ordering process, please open a support ticket through our Discord server for the fastest assistance.
